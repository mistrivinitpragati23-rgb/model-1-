❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Our standard delivery time within the campus and surrounding student housing areas is typically 20-30 minutes. During peak hours (lunch and dinner rushes), it may take up to 45 minutes. You can track your order status live from your account dashboard.

We accept a variety of payment methods to make it easy for students, including:
  • Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, RuPay)
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Student Campus Cards (if linked)
  • Cash on Delivery (CoD)

You can cancel or modify your order within 5 minutes of placing it without any charges. Once the restaurant starts preparing your food, cancellations are no longer possible. To cancel, go to "My Orders" and click the "Cancel" button next to your active order.

If your order is missing items, incorrect, or severely delayed, please reach out to our support team immediately. You can use the live chat feature in the app, or email us at support@studentfoodcorner.com. We will arrange a replacement or process a refund based on the situation.
