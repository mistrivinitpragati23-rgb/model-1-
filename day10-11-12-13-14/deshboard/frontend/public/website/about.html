About FoodieHaven

Fresh Food, Fast Delivery, Built by Students.

Our Story

Started in 2026 as a campus project at Silver Oak University, FoodieHaven has grown into a passion for connecting people with great local flavors.

We focus on three simple principles:

  • 🍔 Quality: Sourcing fresh ingredients daily from certified farms.
  • ⚡ Speed: Fast, reliable delivery straight to your dormitory.
  • 🤝 Community: Partnering with top local chefs and bakers.
